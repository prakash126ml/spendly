Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expenses and spending summaries.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the Service.
  • To send essential service communications, such as password reset emails.

We will never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorised personnel only. We apply industry-standard measures — including encryption at rest and in transit — to protect your information.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may use a limited number of trusted service providers (such as hosting infrastructure) who process data on our behalf and are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.

Any third-party service we use is selected to comply with applicable data protection laws, and they are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for any processing based on your consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact information available on our website.

We are committed to resolving any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
